# Privacy Policy - SortLeads

Last updated: [Today's Date]

## What We Collect

### Files You Upload
- We temporarily store your lead spreadsheet to process it
- Files are deleted after 30 days automatically
- We do not use your data to train AI models
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties

### Payment Information
- Credit card data is processed by Stripe (stripe.com)
- We never see or store your credit card numbers
- Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified

### What We Don't Collect
- ❌ No user accounts or passwords
- ❌ No email addresses (unless you provide them in your spreadsheet)
- ❌ No tracking cookies or analytics
- ❌ No phone numbers or personal identification

## How We Use Your Data

1. **To process your leads:** We send company names and contact info to 
   Claude AI (Anthropic) to score leads
2. **To generate results:** We create a spreadsheet with scored leads
3. **For accounting:** We keep payment records (required by law)

That's it. We don't use your data for anything else.

## Data Security

- All files encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- All connections use HTTPS/TLS 1.3
- Files auto-delete after 30 days
- No backups of your lead data
- Replit provides SOC 2 Type II infrastructure

## Third-Party Services

We use these services to operate:

- **Anthropic (Claude AI):** Processes lead data to generate scores. 
  [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy)
- **Stripe:** Processes payments. 
  [Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy)
- **Replit:** Hosts our application. 
  [Replit Privacy Policy](https://replit.com/site/privacy)

## Your Rights

### Delete Your Data
Your files are automatically deleted after 30 days. If you want immediate 
deletion, email hello@sortleads.io with your session ID.

### Access Your Data
You downloaded your results. That's all we have. No account data exists.

### Questions
Email: hello@sortleads.io
